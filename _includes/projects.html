Music Recommendation System

Collaborated with a team to develop HyG-Con, a hybrid recommendation model that combines knowledge graph-based genre transitions with clustering techniques to enhance cross-genre music discovery. The system outperformed baseline models by generating playlists that balance diversity and coherence, offering listeners smoother transitions and richer exploration beyond typical “filter bubbles.”

Global Art Exploration and Analysis

Collaborated on a project integrating PostgreSQL, Neo4j, and Redis with datasets from MoMA, PainterPalette, WikiData, and Google Trends to analyze artistic influence and public engagement. Our findings revealed unexpected cross-movement popularity correlations, ripple effects from artist passings, and distinct cultural engagement patterns during crises.
